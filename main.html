Shimla

Shimla is the capital of Himachal Pradesh, a state in northern India. It's also known as Simla, which was its official name until 1972. Shimla is a popular tourist destination with colonial history, lakes, snow-capped mountains, and colonial houses. It's also the main cultural, commercial, and educational center of Himachal Pradesh.
